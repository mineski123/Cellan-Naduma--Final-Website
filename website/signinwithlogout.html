Wingman's Special

Spicy Buffalo
Flavor

Buffalo chicken wings are a popular appetizer known for their bold and zesty flavors.
They consist of chicken wings that are deep-fried until crispy, then tossed in a tangy and spicy sauce.
The sauce, commonly referred to as buffalo sauce, is a combination of hot sauce, melted butter, and other
seasonings. The wings are typically served with celery sticks and a creamy dipping sauce like blue
cheese or ranch dressing. The buffalo sauce adds a fiery kick to the chicken wings, while
the crispy skin provides a satisfying crunch.
